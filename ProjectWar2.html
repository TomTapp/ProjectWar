Project War

WAR!

The objective of WAR is to collect all of the cards:


Step 1. Shuffle the deck, distribute the cards evenly between 2-4 players.

Step 2. All players play their top card face up then all cards are compared.

Step 3. The player with the highest card collects all cards played that round into their own shuffle pile.


Note: If there is a tie, the tying players place three cards face down then repeat step 2 & 3.
Note: When you run out of cards in your draw pile, shuffle your shuffle pile and it becomes your draw pile.
